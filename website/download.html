WUPHF Desktop

Your whole office of AI employees in a native window — no browser tab, no terminal. Same office as the CLI, just a real app.

Download Pick your platform below

macOS YOU

Universal — Apple Silicon & Intel · macOS 11+ · signed & notarized by Apple

Download .dmg
  1. Open the downloaded WUPHF-mac.dmg.
  2. Drag WUPHF into your Applications folder.
  3. Launch it from Applications or Spotlight — your office opens in the app.
No scary warnings It's notarized by Apple, so it opens straight away — no "unidentified developer" prompt.

Windows YOU

Windows 10/11 · 64-bit · WebView2 (built in to Windows)

Download installer
  1. Run the downloaded WUPHF-windows-setup.exe.
  2. Follow the installer, then launch WUPHF from the Start menu.
What you'll see — and why The Windows app isn't code-signed yet (signing is on the way), so Windows will warn you. It's expected and safe:
  1. Your browser may flag the download as "not commonly downloaded" → choose Keep.
  2. Windows shows "Windows protected your PC" (SmartScreen) → click More info, then Run anyway.

Prefer the terminal? Or on Linux?

The desktop app and the CLI are the same office — use whichever you like. The CLI runs everywhere, including Linux:

npx wuphf@latest

A native Linux desktop build is on the roadmap; for now Linux runs via the CLI above (your office opens at localhost:7891).
